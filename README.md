# GigShield
### AI-Powered Income Protection for Delivery Workers

GigShield is a parametric insurance platform that protects delivery workers from income loss caused by external disruptions like extreme weather or pollution.

Instead of filing manual claims, the system automatically detects disruptions and triggers compensation payouts.

## Problem

Millions of delivery workers rely on daily earnings from platforms like Swiggy, Zomato, and Amazon.

However, disruptions such as:

- Heavy rain
- Extreme heat
- Floods
- Severe pollution
- Zone shutdowns

can stop deliveries completely.

When workers cannot deliver, they **lose their daily income with no protection.**

## Solution

GigShield provides **AI-powered parametric insurance** that automatically compensates workers when disruptions occur.

Example flow:

Worker registers  
→ AI calculates risk  
→ Weekly insurance plan activated  
→ Weather disruption detected  
→ Claim automatically triggered  
→ Worker receives payout

## Key Features

• Worker onboarding & insurance subscription  
• AI-based risk assessment  
• Dynamic weekly premium calculation  
• Automatic disruption detection  
• Zero-touch claim processing  
• Instant payout simulation  

## Parametric Triggers

Claims activate automatically when conditions exceed thresholds.

| Event | Trigger |
|------|-------|
Heavy Rain | Rainfall > 60mm |
Extreme Heat | Temperature > 45°C |
Severe Pollution | AQI > 400 |
Flood Alert | Official warning |
